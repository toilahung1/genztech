### Key Takeaways for AI Prompt

- **Good CTR:** > 1.5% (Traffic), > 2.5% (Leads)
- **Good CVR (Leads):** > 8.5%
- **Good ROAS (eCommerce):** >= 3x
- **Warning CPM (VN):** > 100,000 VND
- **Warning CTR:** < 0.8%
- **Warning ROAS:** < 1.5x
- **Low Daily Budget:** < 100,000 VND
